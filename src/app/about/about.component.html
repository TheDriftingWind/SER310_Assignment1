With 80% of the population having quirks, U.A. High School is an academy where students can learn and train to become the next generation of heros that will use their quirks for the greater good. U.A. is the #1 top ranked high school for heroics and is considered to be the top Hero Academy in Japan. At U.A. High School, students will learn to be well rounded and capable heros that will be qualified to use the powers in public spaces to defend the public from villans and various dangers.

U.A. has exceptional security measures to ensure that it's students are safe from unwelcome visitors and villians. U.A. is also equipped with several facilities that will aid in developing each student's individual and unique quirk.

Meet All Might

By attending U.A. High School, you may have the chance to meet or even have a class with the No. 1 Hero, All Might himself.

All Might is the barer of the title of the world's Symbol of Peace. He has earned this title through his strength in deterring crime. His very presence in society discourages villians. All Might teaches Foundational Hero Studies at U.A. High School.

Meet Our Students

Meet Izuku. He is a student who attend U.A. High School in Class A. He was thought by many to be born quirkless. However, he drew the attention of the school when he displayed incredible strength by annihalating a training bot at the entrance exam for U.A. High School; saving fellow student Uraraka in the process. He displays a true heart of a hero. Follow him as he begins his journey through U.A.
